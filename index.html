I am a researcher and PhD student at the Visual Recognition Group (VRG) at FEE, Czech Technical University in Prague, advised by prof. Ing. Jiří Matas Ph.D.. My research interest lies in the application of machine learning to visual recognition tasks.
For latest updates about my research, check out the Projects & Papers tab.


When I’m not doing research, I coach a floorball team (Sokol Královské Vinohrady) in the Czech highest league. I also enjoy reading anything between psychology and epic fantasy.
